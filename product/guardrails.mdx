## Narrow by design

Fueling Optimizer should stay tightly focused on single-session fueling decisions.

That constraint is a product strength. It keeps the experience precise and prevents the product from drifting into vague wellness or coaching claims.

## Explicit exclusions

The current version does not cover:

- multi-day fueling plans
- season-long nutrition planning
- medical recovery protocols
- injury or gastrointestinal diagnosis
- adaptive coaching based on historical behavior

## Communication rules

When writing product copy or documentation:

- describe recommendations as deterministic
- explain the relevant inputs
- prefer practical units such as `g`, `ml`, and `mg`
- avoid anthropomorphic or coaching language
- avoid implying clinical or medical advice

## Trust model

Users should understand why a recommendation exists. If a recommendation cannot be explained clearly, it likely does not belong in this product.
